What We Do

Our Services

01
🎼
Music Composition
From a single haunting piano line to a full 80-piece orchestral score, we compose original music built around your narrative. Every cue is written with intention — to guide your audience through the emotional arc of your story without them even realising it's happening.

We work across film, TV, web series, documentaries, and brand films, matching our compositional voice to your genre, tone, and vision. Classical roots. Modern execution. Limitless range.
Film Scores TV Background Music Orchestral Ambient Theme Songs
02
✍️
Lyrics Writing
A great lyric is a great poem in disguise. We write words that breathe — that feel lived-in, personal, and universal all at once. Whether you need a Hindi film ballad, an English pop hook, or a title track that defines an entire project, we craft lyrics that stick long after the music fades.

We write across languages, moods, and meters — from intricate literary prose to punchy commercial hooks — always in service of the song's deeper truth.
Hindi Lyrics English Pop Film Songs Commercial Jingles Title Tracks
03
🎛️
Music Production
Production is where a song truly comes alive. We build immersive sonic worlds — layering textures, designing sounds, and sculpting every frequency until the track breathes exactly the way you imagined it.

Our production work bridges India's rich classical heritage with the pulse of contemporary global pop. We handle everything in-house: arrangement, recording, mixing, and final delivery — giving you a studio-quality product without the studio runaround. Genre is a starting point. We go wherever the song needs to go.
Beat Production Sound Design Mixing & Mastering Arrangement Recording Sync Licensing
